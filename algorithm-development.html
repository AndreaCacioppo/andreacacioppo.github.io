Algorithm development

I can help you use your data for something useful, either by implementing an existing algorithm or by developing a custom one.

Initial Assessment

We start together by evaluating your requirements, so that I can understand your goals and the data you have. By doing this I can get a clear understanding of what you need and can tailor my approach accordingly.

Design and Planning

I will research similar challenges faced by others and study the relevant literature. The goal is to explore the possibility of using well-established solutions to save money and time. If not possible, I will work to develop a custom solution.

Implementation

In this phase, I will propose you a plan that includes an estimation of costs and time. I will also be able to give you a rough estimate of the expected performances and functionalities of the model, to decide whether or not to continue.
