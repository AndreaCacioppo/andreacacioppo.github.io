Legal document analysis using a LLM

Overview

Analyzing a large volume of legal documents is a critical task for law firms and corporate legal teams, often requiring significant time and effort. Leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) through APIs can expedite this process by extracting key information and identifying anomalies in the data.

Problem

A client in the legal industry needed a solution to process and analyze thousands of complex legal contracts efficiently. They sought a system that could extract relevant clauses, identify compliance risks, and detect potential anomalies across the dataset.

Solution

To address this challenge, I developed a system using a Large Language Model API. This system utilized advanced natural language processing techniques to extract important legal clauses, flag inconsistencies, and perform anomaly detection. The framework was customized to meet the client's specific operational and regulatory needs, significantly reducing manual review times.
