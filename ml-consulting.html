ML consulting

I can help you navigate the edge between research and industry, identifying valuable innovations and filtering out the noise. My consulting services are designed to provide strategic guidance and technical expertise to help you leverage machine learning effectively.

Strategic Discovery

We begin by thoroughly understanding your business objectives, current challenges, and available data. This phase is crucial for identifying how ML can deliver tangible value and aligning our strategy with your goals.

Solution & Feasibility Design

Based on the discovery phase, I will research and propose potential ML solutions. This includes assessing the feasibility of different approaches, outlining a roadmap, and defining key performance indicators for success.

Guidance & Oversight

Whether you have an in-house team or need direction on development, I provide expert guidance on model selection, data preprocessing, feature engineering, and best practices for implementation, ensuring your project stays on track.

Evaluation & Iteration

After implementation, it's vital to evaluate model performance against real-world data and business objectives. I assist in setting up monitoring frameworks and advise on iterative improvements to ensure long-term success and adaptability.
